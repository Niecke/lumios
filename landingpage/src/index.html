Lumios is a photo library platform for professional photographers to share curated collections with customers through secure, private links.

Get started

Why Lumios?

🔒

Private by design

Customers access their photos via unique share links. No account required, no personal data collected.

🖼

Watermarked previews

Originals are never exposed. Customers browse watermarked previews and thumbnails.

⭐

Customer selections

Customers browse the gallery and mark their favorite photos directly in the browser.

🔔

Instant notifications

Get notified as soon as your customer finishes their selection, so you can deliver right away.

⚡

Simple & fast

Upload your library, generate a share link, and send it to your customer. That's it.

🔐

Secure infrastructure

Hosted on Google Cloud with encrypted storage, secure sessions, and Cloudflare protection.

How it works

1

Upload

Upload your photos to a library. Lumios generates watermarked previews and thumbnails automatically.

2

Share

Generate a unique, private link and send it to your customer.

3

Select

Your customer browses the gallery and marks their favorites.

4

Deliver

Get notified when the selection is complete and deliver the originals.
